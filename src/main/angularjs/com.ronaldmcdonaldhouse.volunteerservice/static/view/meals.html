It is comforting for families staying at the Ronald McDonald House to come ìhomeî to a hot home-cooked meal after a long day at the hospital with their critically ill child. Groups can choose to serve breakfast or dinner for about 50 people.

Your group is asked to supply all the food, paper products, plastic ware, drinks and cups. If you would like more information about this opportunity, please click on the following link access our online meal calendar to schedule a meal.
